Summer Homework Essay Prompts (Reference)

City of Ember: Journal entry about why Ember was built OR Persuasive on critical thinking OR Expository on curiosity OR Personal narrative.

The Rifle: Destiny vs chance + author’s view on guns OR Message on gun ownership OR Tragic event lesson OR Personal responsibility narrative.

Sammy Keyes: Explain clues and how Sammy solves the hotel thief mystery (5-paragraph essay).

Smiles to Go: Competition good/bad with chess examples OR Personal competition story.

Time Travelling with a Hamster: Compare Pye & Byron’s views on time travel + Al’s final opinion.

The Winter Room: Describe seasons on the farm OR Farm vs city OR Themes (family, work ethic, etc.) OR Personal appreciation experience.

Woodsong: Lessons from books vs nature (Wordsworth quote) with examples from the book.
